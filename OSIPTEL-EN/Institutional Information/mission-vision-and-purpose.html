Mission, Vision and Purpose

Mission

"To regulate and supervise the telecommunications market in order to promote competition, service quality and the respect for users' rights”.

Vision

"To succeed in having accessible and quality telecommunications services provided in a framework of effective competition and to be recognized by the population as an autonomous, technical and innovative institution.”

Purpose

OSIPTEL was created in order to guarantee the quality and efficiency of the telecommunications service provided to users and in order to protect the telecommunications public services market from practices contrary to free and fair competition.
