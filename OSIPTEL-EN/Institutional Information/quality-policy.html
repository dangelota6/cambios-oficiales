Quality policy

"In the OSIPTEL we are committed to improve the satisfaction of users of public telecommunications services, increasing levels of competition in telecommunications markets, improving institutional management benchmarks; and compliance with the requirements of System Quality Management, counting with competent and motivated staff to achieve corporate objectives, based on our vision, mission and values."

Approved by Resolución de Presidencia N° 031-2015-PD/OSIPTEL.
